Resources

Thanks for stopping by!  As a thank you for checking out my site, I’d like to offer you a few free resources that can help your organization strategize and plan for your next winning written proposal or grant request. Please feel free to reach out to me directly if you’d like additional information.

Anatomy of a Proposal

General outline for how to organize a carefully crafted written proposal to an individual or a foundation. Provides tips on what to include in each section and how to frame up a case for support for your proposed project or program.

Proposal Writing Process

Internal document to help the development team recognize their roles/responsibilities for successfully delivering a well-written proposal. This document can also be used for writing stewardship reports.

Success Equation Exercise

This exercise is helpful to use when determining your communications strategy. It helps target the key elements of the organization’s purpose and mission. This can also be used for an organization’s specific program or project. Once this is clear, all communications pieces should support this success equation.
